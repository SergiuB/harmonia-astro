What defines me in my work with people is the ability to listen even beyond words. I believe that gentle touch, offered with love and presence, can bring relief even to the deepest wounds. For me, the feeling of calm and safety—like the caress from a grandmother’s lap—and pure love, without attachment, are the true remedies for the soul’s suffering.

From childhood, I began to discover and use the gifts I received from God. Over time, I learned to listen—not just to hear—and to see—not just to look.

I have an integrative and intuitive therapeutic approach. The knowledge and experiences gained through formal training and advanced courses support and structure the intuitive way I use my tools.

I work with what surrounds me—with people, plants, and animals; with light, air, water, metal, and wood; but above all, with love, prayer, and presence.
