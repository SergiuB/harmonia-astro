Elena Furfurică is a physiotherapist and a Yoga and Pilates Mat & Reformer instructor.

For five years, she was guided on the path of Yoga, Zen, and Budo Zen by professor Mario Vasilescu, founder of the National Group for the Study and Practice of Yoga.

She completed a two-year training in Rhythmical Massage according to Dr. Ita Wegman, co-founder of Anthroposophic Medicine alongside Rudolf Steiner.

Elena is also specialized in prenatal and postnatal gymnastics.

Since 2017, she has been practicing the Japanese martial art Ki-Aikido.
