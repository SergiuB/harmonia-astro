Vasile Scarpet is an Ayurveda lecturer, consultant, and therapist, with a practice dedicated to traditional Indian medicine and holistic balancing principles.

He has been studying Ayurveda since 2005 within A.M.N. Romania (The Association for the Promotion of Non-Conventional Medicines), continuously deepening his understanding of how nutrition, lifestyle, and specific therapies can support harmony between body, mind, and emotions.

Since 2012, he has been offering seminars and educational gatherings dedicated to Ayurvedic knowledge, covering subjects such as the fundamental Ayurvedic constitutions — Vata, Pitta, and Kapha, Ayurvedic nutrition, the subtle effects of tastes and spices, as well as the integration of Ayurvedic practices into daily life.

In his practice, he offers personalized Ayurvedic consultations and private Ayurvedic massage sessions, adapted to the constitution and needs of each individual.

His approach focuses on supporting the body’s natural balance through personalized lifestyle, nutrition, and complementary therapy recommendations, in a gentle, practical, and deeply grounded way connected to the traditional principles of Ayurveda.
